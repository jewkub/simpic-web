Registration

There are two ways to participate in SIMPIC 2019.

  1. If you want to challenge yourself and compete with medical students from all over the world, you can talk it out with your friends and join us as a team of 4 medical students and 1 (optional) advisor. Maximum of two representative teams per medical school are allowed to register for the competition.
  2. If you are interested in SIMPIC, but not in the competition, you can join us as an observer to root for the teams from your school, take part in memorable activities, and bring lots of smiles back home :)

Only around 50 teams will be selected to join the competition based on their responses on short essays and motivation letters.

An observer can be anyone at all and doesn't have to belong to any medical school.

Registration and payment will be done online. Each team or observer is required to sign up an account. Once you create your account, sign in and fill out the application. Please remember your username and password as you will have to log in to your account again during the payment period. One account is only for one team or one observer.



Documents

In order to complete your application, you are required to upload these three documents.

Letter of Approval
A statement verifying your status as representatives from your medical school. You will need approval to confirm that your team is the official representative of your school. Download form

The file should be uploaded in pdf or jpg format
Motivation Letter A motivation letter expressing goals for joining the competition. Please tell us why you want to be a part of SIMPIC family and how this would enhance your international experience.

The file should be uploaded in pdf or jpg format

Fees

Team
Early period: Oct 22 - Nov 04, 2018 310 USD* or 10,500 THB* per team
Regular period: Nov 05 - Nov 22, 2018 340 USD* or 11,500 THB* per team

*Registration fee excludes the accommodation.
Observer
200 USD* or 6,600 THB* per person

*Registration fee excludes the accommodation.

The registration fees cover the following expenses:
  1. Sightseeing
  2. All meals and snacks (except breakfast)
  3. Transportation between Royal River Hotel and Siriraj Hospital
  4. Workshops and recreations
  5. Cultural and other interesting activities
The payment must be made via PayPal or international money transfer during the payment period (Payment confirmation on the official website within 10 days upon the proof of payment submission). Late payment may lead to disqualification.
